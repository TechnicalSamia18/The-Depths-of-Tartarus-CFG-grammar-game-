THE DEPTHS OF TARTARUS

Speak the ancient runic tongue to crack the celestial gates.

You are an ancient Demon imprisoned in a layered celestial dungeon. You cannot strike or shoot. Your only weapon is language. Type incantations into the console to unleash spells. Valid syntax deals devastating damage; incorrect grammar causes the Holy Light to burn you.

Incantation Rules:
  • Spells begin with elements (e.g. IGNIS, AQUA, TERRA) or nested commands.
  • Format: invoke(SPELL). You can nest them: invoke(invoke(IGNIS)).
  • Fury chains are linked with + and must end with !.
  • Example: invoke(invoke(IGNIS) + AQUA) + TERRA!
